## Accelerated option
Given the demonstrated 60cp/semester capacity, remaining 252cp could compress into 2027 S1 – 2028 S2 (4 semesters at ~60cp, front-loading electives once their prereq unit lands) instead of 6, finishing roughly a year early (~end of 2028 vs end of 2029). Would need the actual work placement (EGB499) organised and completed earlier too, since that's a real-world constraint, not just a coursework one. Worth deciding intentionally rather than defaulting into it — flag this if/when planning 2027 S1 enrolment.
