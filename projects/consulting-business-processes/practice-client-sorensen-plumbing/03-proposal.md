## What We Found
Every enquiry into the business goes through one mobile number, with no automation of any
kind. When Rick's on the tools, calls go to voicemail and get returned "when he gets a
chance" — often hours later, sometimes the next day. He estimates a few jobs a month go
quiet because the customer calls someone else first.

## What's Recommended
**AI Lead Response + Follow-Up System**

- Auto-replies to new enquiries within 60 seconds — customer isn't left wondering if anyone
  got their message
- Asks the right qualifying questions for new contacts (job type, location, urgency) so Rick
  calls back already knowing what the job is
- Recognizes existing/repeat customers and skips straight to notifying Rick — no robotic
  "please answer these questions" treatment for people he already knows
- Notifies Rick immediately with a clear summary — he only picks up the phone for a
  qualified, interested customer
- Automatic follow-up after 24 hours if the customer hasn't heard back
