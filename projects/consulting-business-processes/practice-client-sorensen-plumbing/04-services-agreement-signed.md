## Agreement

Between: James Heathcote ("Consultant")
And: Sorensen Plumbing & Gas ("Client"), represented by Rick Sorensen

Date: 2026-07-22

### 1. Scope of Services
Consultant will design, build, and deliver: **AI Lead Response + Follow-Up System**, as
described in the Proposal dated 2026-07-21, including:
- 60-second auto-reply to new enquiries
- Qualifying questions for new contacts
- Existing-customer recognition (skips qualification, direct owner notification)
- Instant owner notification with lead summary
- 24-hour automatic follow-up if no reply

Anything outside this list is a change request, quoted separately.

### 2. Fees & Payment
- Setup fee: $1,800, 50% ($900) due on signing, 50% ($900) due on delivery
- Ongoing fee: $300/month, billed monthly in advance, starting on go-live date (2026-07-29)
- Late payment: 7-day grace period, then service paused until paid

### 3. Timeline
Target delivery: 5 business days from receipt of completed intake form (2026-07-22).

### 4. Client Responsibilities
Client will provide, in a timely manner:
- The mobile number the system will monitor/respond through
- Accurate information about business tone, common questions, and existing customers
- Availability for a short onboarding call and a delivery/handover call

### 5. Intellectual Property
Consultant retains ownership of the underlying system, templates, and code architecture.
Client owns their own business data (customer list, message content specific to their
business) and has a perpetual license to use the delivered system for their business.

### 6. Confidentiality
Both parties agree to keep the other's business information confidential, except as needed
to deliver/receive the service.

### 7. Liability
Consultant's liability is limited to fees paid in the preceding 3 months. Consultant is not
liable for indirect or consequential loss. This is a support tool, not a guarantee of sales
outcomes.

### 8. Term & Termination
- Minimum term: 3 months, then month-to-month
- Either party may terminate with 30 days' written notice
- On termination, Consultant provides a handover per the offboarding process and stops
  billing from the effective termination date
