**James:** "Hey, is this Sorensen Plumbing? This is a cold call — do you wanna hang up now, or
give me 30 seconds. Up to you."

**Rick:** "...nah go on, I've got a sec."

**James:** "Cheers — I'm James, I help local trade businesses with how they handle incoming
enquiries. Quick one — when someone calls or messages you for a quote and you're on a job or
after hours, what actually happens to that enquiry?"

**Rick:** "Goes to voicemail most of the time. I check it when I get a chance, sometimes not
till that night."

**James:** "Right. Do you use anything automatic for that — like a chatbot, auto-reply, booking
system?"

**Rick:** "Nah, nothing like that. It's just my mobile. Always has been."

**James:** "So if someone messages at 6pm tonight, when would they actually hear back?"

**Rick:** "Depends. Could be that night, could be tomorrow arvo if I'm flat out."

**James:** "Roughly how many enquiries would you say you miss or lose because of the timing?"

**Rick:** "Couldn't tell ya exactly. A few a month probably just go quiet, I figure they rang
someone else."

**James:** "Fair enough. Last one — do you use AI anywhere in the business at all? Scheduling,
invoicing, marketing, follow-ups, anything automated?"

**Rick:** "Ha, no. I write me jobs in a diary. Invoices are a Word doc I email when I remember.
Never touched any of that stuff."
