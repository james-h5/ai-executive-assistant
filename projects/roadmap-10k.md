# Roadmap to $10k/Month

**Goal:** $10k/month from AI consulting + futures trading — location-independent
**Why that number:** Funds long-term travel (Japan-style trips, 1-2 months at a time) without needing the bar or tutoring

**Realistic timeline: 15–18 months** (October 2027 — January 2028)
Best case if things move fast: mid-2027. Honest case: end of 2027.

---

## The Model at Scale

Consulting alone at $300/month retainer won't get you there — you'd need 33 clients. The path is:

- **Start cheap to get proof** → raise prices as results come in
- **Target: 10–12 clients at $600–800/month** = $6,000–9,600/month recurring
- **Trading fills the gap** to $10k and eventually becomes its own leg

---

## Phase 1 — First Money (Now → September 2026)

**Goal:** 1–3 paying clients. Proof the system works. First trading payout.

### Consulting
- [ ] Update pitch to address returning-caller objection
- [ ] Send cold DMs to all 20–30 prospects on the list
- [ ] Offer first client a heavily discounted or free setup in exchange for a testimonial and case study
- [ ] Land 1–2 paying clients at $300/month retainer
- [ ] Document results (response time, leads qualified, owner time saved)

### Trading
- [ ] Hit consistency target (define what "consistent" means for your funded account rules)
- [ ] Pass payout threshold — first withdrawal by end of 2026

### Income target by end of Phase 1
| Stream | Monthly |
|---|---|
| Consulting retainers (1–2 clients) | $300–600 |
| Trading | $0–500 |
| **Total (location-independent)** | **$300–1,100** |

*Bartending + tutoring still running in the background — fund life while this builds.*

## Phase 2 — Repeatability (October 2026 → February 2027)

**Goal:** 4–6 clients. Proven case study. Referrals starting. Prices rising.

### Consulting
- [ ] Case study written and in the portfolio
- [ ] Referral ask to all existing clients ("who else do you know who runs a trade business?")
- [ ] Raise retainer to $400–500/month for new clients
- [ ] Build second service offering: e.g. AI booking/reminder system, or quote-follow-up automation
- [ ] Post content or case study to Skool / Instagram — start getting inbound

### Trading
- [ ] First payout received
- [ ] Contributing $500–1,500/month

### Income target by end of Phase 2
| Stream | Monthly |
|---|---|
| Consulting retainers (4–6 clients) | $1,600–3,000 |
| Trading | $500–1,500 |
| **Total (location-independent)** | **$2,100–4,500** |

*This is where location-independence becomes viable — you could do a short trip and keep this running.*

## Phase 3 — Scale (March 2027 → August 2027)

**Goal:** 8–12 clients. $5–8k/month. Systems in place so you're not doing everything manually.

### Consulting
- [ ] Systemise delivery — templated builds, documented processes, maybe a VA for admin
- [ ] Price anchor raised to $600–800/month for new clients (existing stay at old rate)
- [ ] Add a third service: AI-generated content automation, or a monthly reporting dashboard
- [ ] Start getting inbound (referrals + content starting to compound)
- [ ] Consider a small waitlist if demand is there — signals value

### Trading
- [ ] Consistent monthly withdrawals
- [ ] Contributing $1,500–3,000/month

### Income target by end of Phase 3
| Stream | Monthly |
|---|---|
| Consulting retainers (8–12 clients) | $4,800–9,600 |
| Trading | $1,500–3,000 |
| **Total (location-independent)** | **$6,300–12,600** |

*This is the $10k zone. You might hit it here or need one more quarter.*

## Phase 4 — $10k Locked In (September 2027 → January 2028)

**Goal:** $10k/month consistently for 3+ months in a row. Stable enough to drop bar work.

### What this looks like
- 10–15 clients at $600–800/month recurring
- Trading pulling $1,500–3,000/month
- You're working ~20–25 hours/week on consulting (delivery + sales)
- You could leave Brisbane for 6 weeks and the income holds

### The milestone check
- [ ] 3 consecutive months above $10k
- [ ] Income doesn't require you to be in Brisbane
- [ ] Bar and tutoring dropped or kept purely by choice

## The Honest Risks

**Consulting risks:**
- Churn — trade businesses close, budgets get cut. Plan for 1 client lost per quarter once you have 5+
- Price resistance — Brisbane trades are cost-conscious. Results data is your weapon against this
- Your time — uni returns mid-July, boxing takes real hours. Pace matters

**Trading risks:**
- Funded accounts have drawdown rules — one bad run can reset progress
- Income is variable month-to-month, not linear
- Don't count on trading until you have 3+ months of consistent withdrawals

**The biggest risk overall:**
Going slow in Phase 1. The case study is everything. One client with strong results unlocks referrals, credibility, higher pricing, and inbound. Delay there delays everything else.

## The Unlock Sequence

```
Cold DMs → First call → First client (discounted) → Results documented
→ Case study → Charge full price → Referrals → More clients
→ Raise prices → Systems → Scale → $10k
```

Every step gates the next. Right now you're between "cold DMs" and "first call." That's where to focus.
