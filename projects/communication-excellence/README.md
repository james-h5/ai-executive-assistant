# Communication Excellence

Develop the ability to express thoughts clearly, confidently, and precisely — in conversation, in writing, and under pressure.

**Status:** Active

## Description

James currently finds it hard to turn his thoughts into words as clearly as he wants to. The gap between what he's thinking and what comes out is the problem. This project is about closing that gap — so he can communicate his ideas with the same clarity they have in his head.

Use the `/comm-coach` skill for practice sessions.

## Focus Areas

- **Articulating ideas clearly** — no vagueness, no trailing off
- **Speaking confidently** — no filler words, no second-guessing mid-sentence
- **Being easy to follow** — logical flow, one point at a time
- **Writing with impact** — emails, DMs, pitches that land

## Why This Matters

Better communication = more effective in every area:
- Pitching consulting clients and closing deals
- Building genuine connections with people
- Expressing ideas in uni and work
- Speaking with locals while travelling

## Inputs James Can Use
- Rough written thoughts or drafts (paste directly)
- Voice memo transcripts (paste the text)
- Something he said that didn't land the way he wanted
- Anything he wants to say but can't find the words for

## Next Steps
- Run first comm-coach session whenever something comes up that needs clarity
- Track patterns in `logs/communication/` — over time, spot recurring issues
- Identify the 1-2 habits to eliminate first (e.g. filler words, burying the point)
