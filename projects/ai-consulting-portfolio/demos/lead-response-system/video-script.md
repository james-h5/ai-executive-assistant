# Demo Video Script — AI Lead Response System
*Target: 90 seconds. Read at a relaxed pace.*

---

[FACE TO CAMERA]

"Most trade businesses lose leads not because they're bad at the job — but because they're on the tools all day and can't pick up the phone.

By the time they call back, the customer has already booked someone else.

This system fixes that."

---

[SHARE SCREEN — show trigger.dev dashboard with the two tasks visible]

"What you're looking at is a live automation I built. The moment a new lead comes in — whether that's a missed call or a website enquiry — this fires automatically."

"I'll simulate a lead right now. Someone's called about a quote, left their number. Watch what happens."

[HIT RUN]

---

[SHOW the task executing — steps completing in the dashboard]

"The system pulls the lead's details, generates a personalised SMS response using AI, and sends it — all within about 60 seconds."

[SHOW phone — lead SMS arrives]

"There it is. The message to the lead it sounds like it came directly from the business owner — asking when's a good time to call. They're not sitting there wondering if anyone got their message."

---

[SHOW phone — owner notification SMS]

"And the owner gets this. Name, number, what they're after. He calls them back when he's free — except now the lead is already warmed up and expecting his call."

---

[FACE TO CAMERA]

"If they don't reply in 24 hours, a follow-up goes out automatically. No manual work. No leads falling through the cracks.

That's what I build for trade businesses in Brisbane."
