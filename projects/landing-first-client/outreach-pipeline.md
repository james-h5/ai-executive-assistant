## Notes

**Patterns:**
- Most Brisbane tradies still rely on phone + email with no automation — the AI lead response pitch lands naturally
- Plumbers show the strongest structural pain: mobiles as primary contact means every on-site job risks a missed enquiry
- HVAC businesses with strict office hours are the easiest pitch — leads calling at 5:01pm are straight to voicemail
- Instagram presence is good for 4 of 10 — DM outreach is viable for those

**Contact first:**
1. **Brisbane City Plumbing and Gas** (@bcpandg) — mobile-only, no form. Every call they miss while on a job is a lost customer. Clear pitch, active Instagram.
2. **Quality Air** (@qualityairconditioning) — shuts at 4pm weekdays. Any lead calling after that disappears. Active Instagram.
3. **Jason's Plumbing** — Mon-Fri 7-5 only. Weekend enquiries are dead without automation. Good fit for the lead response system.

**Outreach approach:**
- Instagram DM for those with handles, email for others
- Lead with the pain: "How many jobs do you lose when you're on-site and can't answer?"
- Follow up with the demo (60-second auto-reply in action)
