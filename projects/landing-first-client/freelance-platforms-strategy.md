# Freelance Platforms Strategy

Secondary channel to [Direct outreach](README.md), aimed at faster cash flow while the Brisbane-trades consultancy pipeline builds. Synthesized from a Claude chat session, 2026-07-18.

**Does not change the locked niche/offer** (Brisbane trades, AI Lead Response + Follow-Up System, $1,500-$2,000 + $300/mo — see `decisions/log.md`, 2026-06-25). This is additive.

**Time budget:** 8-12 hrs/week on top of uni (60cp), boxing, bartending, tutoring. Working assumption — revisit if it proves too aggressive.

## The 5 Pathways (fastest to slowest cash)

### 1. Freelance prompt engineering / AI workflow setup
- Platforms: Fiverr, Upwork
- Rate: $50-150/hr, 8-12 hrs/week = $400-1,800/week
- Advantage: already deep in Claude; most freelancers aren't
- Time to first dollar: 1-2 weeks
- Barrier: low entry, high competition, but quality gap is real

### 2. Lightweight AI audits
- $200-400 per audit, 2-3 hrs each (document current processes, identify 3-5 AI opportunities, deliverable)
- Target: Etsy shops, local service business owners, coaches
- 1-2 audits/week = $400-800/week
- **Doubles as a case study** for the Brisbane-trades consultancy — fills the empty `ai-consulting-portfolio/` gap
- Barrier: sales (cold outreach via email, LinkedIn, local networks)

### 3. AI content creation for creators
- YouTubers, Substack writers, course creators — scripts, thumbnail briefs, SEO, email sequences
- $300-800/month retainer per client; 4-5 clients = $500/week
- Lowest technical lift, portfolio built from own experiments
- Time to first dollar: 2-3 weeks if promotion starts now

### 4. Productized templates
- Package SOP scaffolds into done-for-you templates, $97-297 each
- Sell direct to small biz owners (digital product, no time investment post-launch)
- Examples: "AI for salon scheduling," "AI for plumber invoicing," "AI for gym marketing"
- Scalable but needs marketing; not a first move

### 5. Agency white-labeling / micro-agency partnerships
- Partner with agencies that need AI implementation but lack the skills — they own the client relationship, James handles the AI workflows
- $300-600/week per partner at 2-3 small projects/month
- Barrier: needs pitching partners, less control

## First Moves (running in parallel)
1. Post Fiverr/Upwork gigs for prompt-engineering/workflow-setup work
2. Build the lightweight 3-hour AI audit template (highest leverage — feeds both freelance income and the main consultancy's case studies)
3. Start pitching content creators for scripts/SEO/email work

## Phased Plan (12 weeks)
- **Weeks 1-3:** Post Fiverr/Upwork profiles + gigs, run in parallel with direct outreach
- **Weeks 2-6:** Land 2-3 quick audit gigs ($200-400 each), use learnings to refine the audit template
- **Weeks 6-12:** Convert 1-2 audit clients into $400-500/month retainers
- **Target:** $400-600/week by week 12
