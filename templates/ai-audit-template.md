# Lightweight AI Audit — Process & Deliverable

Sell this as a fixed-price, fixed-scope offer: **"I'll audit your business processes and hand you a report showing exactly where AI can save you time or money — 3 hours, $200-400."**

Total time budget: ~3 hours (30-45 min discovery call, 90 min analysis, 45-60 min writing the report).

---

## 1. Discovery call (30-45 min)

Book a call, record it if the client's OK with it (saves re-asking later). Questions to run through:

- Walk me through a typical week/day in the business — what do you spend the most time on?
- What's the most repetitive or annoying admin task you do?
- Where do leads/enquiries come from, and what happens after someone contacts you?
- What software/tools do you already use? (booking, invoicing, CRM, socials)
- Where do things fall through the cracks — missed calls, late replies, manual data entry, forgotten follow-ups?
- If you could wave a wand and remove one task from your week, what would it be?

**Goal:** leave the call with 3-5 concrete processes to analyse, not vague pain points.

## 2. Analysis (90 min)

For each process identified, work through:

- **Current state** — steps involved, who does it, how long it takes, how often
- **AI opportunity** — is this a fit for automation/AI? (repetitive, rule-based, text/data-heavy = good fit; requires judgment/relationship = poor fit)
- **Effort to fix** — quick win (days), medium (weeks), or bigger project (not in scope for this audit)
- **Estimated impact** — hours saved/week, money saved, leads recovered

Rank the 3-5 opportunities by impact vs. effort. Pick the **top 3** for the report — don't overwhelm the client.

## 3. Deliverable report (45-60 min to write)

Structure — one page per opportunity, plus a summary:

```
# AI Opportunity Audit — [Business Name]
Prepared by [Your Name] | [Date]

## Summary
[2-3 sentences: what you found, headline opportunity, estimated impact]

## Opportunity 1: [Name]
- Current state: [what happens now]
- The problem: [time/money/leads lost]
- Recommendation: [specific AI/automation fix]
- Estimated impact: [X hours/week saved, or $X/month, or X% fewer missed leads]
- Effort to implement: [quick win / medium project]

## Opportunity 2: [Name]
[same structure]

## Opportunity 3: [Name]
[same structure]

## Next steps
If you'd like help implementing any of these, here's what that could look like: [1-2 lines pointing to a paid implementation engagement — this is the upsell]
```

Deliver as a clean PDF or Google Doc. Keep it visual/scannable — bullet points, bold the numbers, no walls of text.

## 4. The upsell

The audit is the wedge, not the endgame. Close with an offer to implement:

- For Brisbane trades leads → point toward the AI Lead Response + Follow-Up System ($1,500-$2,000 + $300/mo)
- For freelance-platform clients → offer a smaller implementation package or ongoing retainer, scoped to what the audit found

## Notes
- First few audits: treat as case-study generation as much as income. Ask permission to use (anonymised or named) results as a portfolio piece for `projects/ai-consulting-portfolio/`.
- If the client's processes don't reveal a clean AI opportunity, be honest — don't force a recommendation that doesn't fit. Credibility compounds.
