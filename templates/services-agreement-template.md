# Services Agreement Template

> **Not legal advice.** This is a working template for a solo consultant's first engagements — before it's used with a real paying client, have a solicitor (or a service like LawPath/Legal123) review it, especially the liability and IP clauses. Cheap insurance against an expensive misunderstanding.

---

## Agreement Document Structure

```
# Services Agreement

Between: James Heathcote ("Consultant") — [ABN if applicable]
And: [Business Name] ("Client"), represented by [Owner Name]

Date: [date]

## 1. Scope of Services
Consultant will design, build, and deliver: [system name — e.g. AI Lead Response + Follow-Up System]
as described in the Proposal dated [date], including:
- [capability 1]
- [capability 2]
- [capability 3]

Anything outside this list is a change request, quoted separately.

## 2. Fees & Payment
- Setup fee: $[amount], due [on signing / 50% on signing + 50% on delivery]
- Ongoing fee: $[amount]/month, billed [monthly, in advance], starting on go-live date
- Late payment: [grace period, e.g. 7 days, then service paused until paid]

## 3. Timeline
Target delivery: [X business days] from receipt of completed intake form.
Delays caused by Client (e.g. late intake info, unavailable for testing) push the timeline accordingly.

## 4. Client Responsibilities
Client will provide, in a timely manner:
- Access to relevant contact channels (phone/email/social) needed for the system to function
- Accurate information about business processes, tone, and existing customers
- Availability for a short onboarding call and a delivery/handover call

## 5. Intellectual Property
Consultant retains ownership of the underlying system, templates, and code architecture.
Client owns their own business data (customer lists, message content specific to their business)
and has a perpetual license to use the delivered system for their business.

## 6. Confidentiality
Both parties agree to keep the other's business information confidential, except as needed
to deliver/receive the service.

## 7. Liability
Consultant's liability is limited to fees paid in the preceding 3 months. Consultant is not
liable for indirect or consequential loss (e.g. lost business from a missed lead the system
didn't catch). This is a support tool, not a guarantee of sales outcomes.

## 8. Term & Termination
- Minimum term: [e.g. 3 months], then month-to-month
- Either party may terminate with [30 days'] written notice
- On termination, Consultant provides a handover per the offboarding process, and stops billing
  from the effective termination date

## 9. Signatures
Consultant: _____________________  Date: _______
Client:     _____________________  Date: _______
```

## Notes
- Keep the real version to 1-2 pages — this is a small trades business, not an enterprise deal. A dense legal document will spook them more than protect you.
- If a client wants to negotiate a clause, that's fine — track what gets negotiated away so patterns show up over multiple clients (e.g. if everyone pushes back on the minimum term, reconsider it).
